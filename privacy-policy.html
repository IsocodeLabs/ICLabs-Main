Privacy Policy

Active Since: June 2025 • Jurisdiction: India

ISCodeLabs has been active since its formation in June 2025. We build premium digital products and platforms. We aren't in the business of collecting, hoarding, or selling user data. This policy lays out what data we collect, why we collect it, and what we do with it in plain, transparent language.

What We Collect

When you contact us, request a quote, or sign up for a build slot, we collect the information you voluntarily provide:

  • Identification Data: Your name.
  • Contact Data: Your email address and WhatsApp/phone number.
  • Project Details: Information about your company, website goals, budget, timeline, and requirements.

Why We Collect It

We use this data to build websites and tools, and to run our systems. Specifically:

  • To respond to your inquiries and evaluate your project.
  • To communicate about project progress, milestones, feedback, and delivery.
  • To improve our own internal software products, templates, and frameworks.

How We Share Data

We do not sell your personal data. We do not spam you. We only share information with third-party tools that are essential to running our business, including:

  • Hosting providers and server infrastructure providers.
  • Privacy-focused web analytics services (to see how visitors interact with our site).
  • Communication systems (email and messaging tools) to coordinate with you.

Cookies

We keep cookies to an absolute minimum. We only use them for basic web analytics to understand traffic patterns and optimize page load speeds. No cross-site ad tracking, no profiling, and no invasive tracking scripts.

Your Rights

It's your data. You have the right to request a copy of the details we hold, request that we update them, or request that we completely delete all your information from our database. If you wish to opt out of any communications, just send us an email.

Contact Us

If you have any questions or would like to request data deletion, email our founders directly at hello@isocodeslab.com.
