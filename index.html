Questions

The answers you need before booking.

What does a premium website cost?

Most projects land between ₹50,000 and ₹1,50,000. Final quote depends on page count, animation depth, content, and integrations.

Do you only build websites?

No. We also build custom web applications, AI integrations, creator product launch systems, and experimental venture prototypes.

Can you handle complex systems like hospital software?

Yes. We've built systems covering appointments, billing, consultations, inventory, dashboards, and role-based workflows. Complex systems are scoped separately.

How long does a project take?

Most websites ship in 6–8 weeks. Complex web apps depend on scope — typically 8–14 weeks after discovery.

Will the animations slow the site down?

No. We optimize every asset, lazy-load animations, and provide a reduced-motion fallback for accessibility.
